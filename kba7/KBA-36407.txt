How can I sort my configurations within DocuWare Configuration?



Question:
How can I sort the configurations on the DocuWare Configuration page?
Answer:
Two types of sorting are offered within DocuWare
Note: The sorting options change between plugin for flexibility.

The below plugins offer sorting capabilities:

Document Processing
Forms
General Mail
Indexing Assistance
File Cabinets
Document Relations
Notifications
Stamps
Document Trays
Smart Connect

KBA applicable for both Cloud and On-premise Organizations.
